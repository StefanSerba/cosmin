💡 Core Principles
1. Eat the rainbow — variety of colours = variety of antioxidants
2. Omega-3s daily — fatty fish, walnuts or flaxseed at every opportunity
3. Gut health first — legumes, fibre and fermented foods reduce inflammation
4. Sleep is medicine — 7–9 hours is non-negotiable for healing
5. Stress spikes inflammation — even 10 min of calm daily helps
